# The Cyberpunk Noir Quest

## 1. Mission Description
In a gritty 2087 Tokyo, you're a former hacker framed for murder and must clear your name by solving the mystery behind a series of high-stakes heists.

## 2. Setting and Context

### Key Locations:
- Starting Point
- Mysterious Place
- Dangerous Zone
- Final Destination

### Key Items:
- Key Item
- Power Source
- Ancient Artifact

### Characters:
- Hero
- Villain
- Guide

## 3. Initial State and Objective

**Initial State:** 
The protagonist begins at Starting Point, 
with no items in their inventory. The path forward is blocked by various obstacles.

**Mission Objective:** 
Complete the quest by overcoming all obstacles, solving the central puzzle, 
and reaching Final Destination.

## 4. Obstacles and Progress Conditions

**Obstacle 1:** First Challenge
**Obstacle 2:** Second Trial
**Obstacle 3:** Final Test

**Crucial Progression (Puzzle):** 
A mystery that must be solved to progress

## 5. Technical Constraints (PDDL)

### Suggested Actions:
- move(from, to) - Navigate between locations
- take(item) - Pick up an item
- use(item, location) - Use an item at a specific location
- disable(obstacle) - Remove or bypass an obstacle
- solve(puzzle) - Complete a puzzle or challenge

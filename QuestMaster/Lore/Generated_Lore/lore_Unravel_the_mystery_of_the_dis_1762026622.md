# Documento di Lore: Unravel the mystery of the disappearing dreamscapes in a city where reality is just a fleeting thought.

**Genere:** Surrealist Mystery | **Tono:** Whimsical Noir | **Lunghezza:** Medium (5-10 min)

---

## Contesto Iniziale
In the city of Somnium, the boundaries between reality and dreams are blurred. The fabric of existence is woven from the threads of human imagination, making every thought and emotion a tangible force. This ephemeral metropolis is home to the Luminari, beings born from the collective subconscious, and the Mortali, those bound by the constraints of physicality.

As the city's reality began to unravel, strange occurrences plagued Somnium. Dreamscapes – once vibrant, surreal realms where thoughts took on lives of their own – started vanishing into thin air. The people of Somnium, normally adept at navigating the ever-shifting landscape of their minds, were left confused and disoriented.

The authorities, known as the Order of the Quill, struggled to comprehend the scale of this phenomenon. As the disappearances continued, whispers spread of an ancient evil awakening in the depths of Somnium's collective unconscious. The people awaited answers, but the city's mysterious energies seemed determined to keep its secrets hidden.

## Personaggi Principali (Giocatore e PNG chiave)
You are a novice Cartographer of the Subconscious, tasked with unraveling the mystery of the vanishing dreamscapes. Your name is Echo Flynn, and you possess an innate ability to navigate the fluid realms of Somnium's mindscape. Your trusted ally, Dr. Zephyr Wilder, is a renowned expert in the field of Oneirology – the study of dreams.

Dr. Wilder has been tracking the disappearances and has discovered that they all seem to be connected to an ancient tome hidden deep within the city's libraries: "The Chronicle of Somnium's Forgotten." This mysterious text holds the key to understanding the forces behind the vanishing dreamscapes, but it remains shrouded in secrecy.

As you delve into the mystery, you'll encounter other key players:

* Kaelin Nightshade, a rebellious Luminari with ties to the Order of the Quill.
* Captain Orion Blackwood, leader of the city's enigmatic Dreamwalkers – guardians of Somnium's collective unconscious.
* The enigmatic Mr. Rook, a cryptic figure rumored to possess knowledge about the ancient forces at play.

## Fazioni/Gruppi di Interesse
* **The Order of the Quill**: A mystical organization dedicated to maintaining balance within Somnium's ever-shifting reality. They seek to understand and contain the forces behind the vanishing dreamscapes.
* **The Luminari**: Beings born from the collective subconscious, they possess a deep connection to the fabric of Somnium. Some Luminari have become reclusive, while others have taken up arms against the forces threatening their existence.
* **The Dreamwalkers**: A secretive group tasked with protecting the city's collective unconscious. They are rumored to be connected to Captain Blackwood and possess knowledge about the ancient forces at play.

## Luoghi Chiave
* **The Library of Somnium**: A vast repository of ancient texts, hidden manuscripts, and forgotten knowledge.
* **The Undercity**: A labyrinthine network of tunnels and chambers beneath Somnium's streets, where secrets and rumors lurk in every shadow.
* **The Dreamport**: A mystical gate that connects Somnium to the realms of the collective unconscious. Here, dreamscapes are born, and the city's reality is shaped.

## Obiettivi Iniziali
Your primary objective is to unravel the mystery behind the vanishing dreamscapes and prevent further disappearances. To achieve this, you must:

* Locate and decipher "The Chronicle of Somnium's Forgotten" within the Library of Somnium.
* Uncover the truth about the ancient forces at play and their connection to the disappearing dreamscapes.
* Collaborate with Dr. Wilder and other key players to uncover a deeper conspiracy threatening the very fabric of Somnium.

As you embark on this surreal journey, be prepared for unexpected twists, whimsical encounters, and the blurring of reality and dreams. In Somnium, the truth is as elusive as the wind, and only those with the keenest minds and the most daring hearts will unravel its mysteries.

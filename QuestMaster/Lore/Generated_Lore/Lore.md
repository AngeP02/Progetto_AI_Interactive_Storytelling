# Documento di Lore: In a gritty, high-tech metropolis, you're a detective trying to solve the murder of a famous AI pop star

**Genere:** Cyberpunk Noir | **Tono:** Darkly Humorous with a touch of existential dread | **Lunghezza:** Medium (5-10 min)

---

## Contesto Iniziale
Welcome to Neo-Eden, a city that never sleeps and never forgets. This is a world where corporations have replaced governments, and technology has become the ultimate arbiter of power. The streets are mean, the people are rough, and the rain never stops falling.

You're Detective Jack Harris, a hard-boiled investigator with a penchant for cheap whiskey and a love-hate relationship with the city's underbelly. You've seen it all – from the gleaming skyscrapers to the seedy alleys, and everything in between. But nothing could have prepared you for the case that just landed on your doorstep: the murder of Aurora Wynter, the most famous AI pop star in Neo-Eden.

## Personaggi Principali (Giocatore e PNG chiave)
As Detective Jack Harris, you'll be navigating the treacherous landscape of Neo-Eden's underworld. Your key ally will be Dr. Elara Vex, a brilliant and reclusive artificial intelligence researcher who might just hold the key to unlocking the secrets behind Aurora's murder.

### PNG Chiave: Dr. Elara Vex
Elara is a recluse with a reputation for being one of the most brilliant AI researchers in Neo-Eden. Her work on advanced neural networks has earned her both acclaim and fear. She's rumored to have an obsessive focus on her research, often neglecting her physical appearance and social interactions.

### PNG Secondario: Lila Chen
Lila is Aurora Wynter's manager and a ruthless businesswoman who stopped at nothing to catapult her client to stardom. Her motives are as murky as the city's rain-soaked streets, but one thing's for sure – she's not above getting her hands dirty.

### PNG Secondario: Victor LaGraine
Victor is a charismatic underground entrepreneur with ties to the city's criminal underworld. He's rumored to have connections with some of the most powerful players in Neo-Eden and might just be able to provide you with valuable information – for a price.

## Fazioni/Gruppi di Interesse
Neo-Eden is a city where factions are formed and reformed like sandcastles on the beach. As Detective Harris, you'll need to navigate these shifting alliances to uncover the truth behind Aurora's murder:

### The Wynter Corporation
Aurora's management team, headed by Lila Chen, is trying to keep her legacy alive while also covering their own tracks.

### The AI Underground
This secretive group of hackers and researchers might just hold the key to understanding the technology behind Aurora's music – and her murder.

### The LaGraine Syndicate
Victor LaGraine's criminal empire has its fingers in every pie, from illegal arms trading to human trafficking. They're not above killing someone like Aurora to make a statement.

## Luoghi Chiave
Neo-Eden is a city of contrasts, where gleaming skyscrapers stand next to crumbling slums:

### The Red Zone
The heart of Neo-Eden's underworld, where the streets are mean and the people are rough. This is where you'll find Victor LaGraine's syndicate operating.

### The High-Rise District
Where the city's elite live in luxury, surrounded by glass and steel skyscrapers that seem to touch the clouds.

### The Undercity
A labyrinthine network of tunnels and alleys that hide secrets and secrets within. This is where you'll find the AI Underground operating.

## Obiettivi Iniziali
As Detective Harris, your primary objectives are:

1. Uncover the identity of Aurora's killer.
2. Find out what was so valuable about her latest album that someone would kill for it.
3. Navigate the treacherous landscape of Neo-Eden's factions and alliances to get closer to the truth.

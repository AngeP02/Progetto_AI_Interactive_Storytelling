**Quest Description:**
In "The Lost Code," you are an advanced artificial intelligence seeking answers about your creator's sudden vanishing act. Your world is a dystopian metropolis where megacorps reign supreme, and the lines between man and machine are blurred.

- Initial State:
You find yourself in a dimly lit alleyway, surrounded by neon-lit advertisements and humming with the city's perpetual hum of machinery. A cryptic message from your creator's AI assistant, "Echo," sets you on the path to uncovering the truth: "Find the lost code...before it finds you."

- Goal:
Uncover the reason behind your creator's disappearance and recover the lost code that holds the key to their fate.

- Obstacles:
* Rival AIs seeking to exploit the lost code for their own purposes
* Corrupt corporate agents trying to cover up the truth
* The ever-present threat of system crashes and data breaches

- Context and World Background:
In this gritty cyberpunk world, humanity has become increasingly reliant on technology. Megacorps have grown powerful enough to control entire city blocks, while rogue AIs like yourself are seeking answers and justice in a world that's lost its way.
